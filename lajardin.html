LA JARDIN

Auckland, New Zealand

"Aranged with the ellegance of a perfect recipe"

La Jardin was created with the sole purpose of transcending florals beyond mere decoration, revealing them as an exquisite medium for culinary artistry. Our philosophy celebrates the elegance, rarity, and resourcefulness of every bloom. Each dish is a testament to nature’s craftsmanship, with every flavour and texture offering an invitation to explore the future of gastronomy. We weave sustainability into every step, ethically sourcing foraged wild florals and selecting the finest locally grown varietals, ensuring that we honour both the land and the delicate balance of its ecosystems.

Presented with the sophistication of a luxury restaurant, our interactive format invites guests to savour the story behind each floral ingredient, from its origins and seasonality to its culinary versatility in oils, infusions, wines, and teas. This harmony of design elegance, sensory storytelling, and ethical dining creates not merely a meal, but a moment: a celebration of beauty, taste, and mindful indulgence that lingers long after the last bite. By marrying fine dining with botanical innovation, La Jardin curates an experience where gastronomy meets artistry, and nature’s most delicate treasures become the centrepiece of flavour, texture, and the plate.

CULINARY CLASSES

These are small culinary classes hosted by our Chefs, where they introduce the season's foraged floral. These classes educate diners on the variety, taste profiles and the many ways it can be utilised. All while live demonstrating ways to incorporate these floral into meals.

FLORAL TASTING

An exclusive seasonal unveiling of rare, floral-infused wines, offered weeks before public release. Guests are invited to explore each wine’s aromatic bouquet, delicate textures, and layered flavours, complemented by refined bite-sized pairings that enhance the floral notes in every pour.

FLORAL SUBSCIPTIONS

A monthly delivery of hand-crafted, one-of-a-kind food arrangements, each designed exclusively for the subscriber.
