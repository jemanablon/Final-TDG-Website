Culinary Classes

Each season, La Jardin invites members to an intimate culinary workshop, where our Chefs collaborate with guests to create exquisite dishes infused with the flavors, colors, and aromas of seasonal blooms. These hands-on sessions celebrate the artistry of cooking with flowers from learning about rare varietals and their flavor profiles, to experimenting with unique pairings such as edible petals, foraged wild florals, and gold leaf, adorned garnishes. A sensory journey where seasonality meets creativity, offering participants a chance to craft one-of-a-kind floral-inspired dishes alongside our chefs.
