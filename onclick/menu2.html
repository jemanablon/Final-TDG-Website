DAHLIA PINNATA

Dahlia Pinnata commonly known as "Dahlia" is a floral native to Central America. Dahlias symbolises dignity and elegance hence they have been used as decorative element in cusine for many years . In La Jardin we aimed to use Dahlias not just as finishing touches but the main source of flavour and texture in our dishes. From it's tubers to it's petals, our chefs and researches have worked hard to encoperate and showcase Dahlias.

Flavor Profile

  • Tart : similar to Potatoes and Radish
  • Nutty
  • Sligtly floral : Depending on how it is prepared and used
  • Peppery
