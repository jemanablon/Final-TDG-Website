HIBISCUS SABDARIFFA

Hibiscus Sabdariffa, comonly known as "Roselle" amd "Jamaica flower", originated in the countries of Africa & Southeast Asia. Hibiscus is known for it's ability to lower body temperature hence it is encoperated in drinks such as aguas frescas and soreel drinks in hot countries such as Egypt and Mexico. This floral is also valued in traditional Chinese Medicine for its antioxidant and digestive properties.
